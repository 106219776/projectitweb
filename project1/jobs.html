IT Support Technician

Refrence number: IT123

Overview

Providing direct technical support for staff devices, accounts, and office systems. You will also be logging, trouble shooting and ensuring minimal time lost by technical issues.

Job type: Full time

Key Responsibilities

  • Be ready for on-call and walk-up issues
  • Documenting all changes and support on company issued app
  • Install, configure, and maintain laptops, printers, and other machinery if needed
  • Resolving issues and taking clear steps and diagnostics to prevent repeated issue

Required Qualifications

Essential (ordered by priority)

  1. At least 3+ college years in Computer Science
  2. Working knowledge of Windows 10/11 and basic networking (TCP/IP, DNS, ...)
  3. Clear communications and problem-solving mindset
  4. Basic familiarity with apps such as Github and Jira

Non essential but preferable

  • Ability to freely intergrate with employee group
  • Scripting basics for automation

Salary range: 10M montly

Above is expected salary when applying, salary is expected to change after the first month depending on ability and efficency, wage raise applications are also available to be filed after four month period.

Report to: Team leader

Be available on call during non-working hours

Cybersecurity Specialist

Refrence number: CS123

Overview

Protecting the company, employee and customer's application from unwanted malware and cyber threats. You will be in charge of monitoring to detect and respond to any security breaches while mainatining optimal standards

Job type: Full time

Key Responsibilities

  • Updating and maintaining the network's security and integrity
  • Monitor to detect potential threats and attacks
  • Training employees on proper safe cybersecurity practices
  • Develop and enforce security policies, guidelines and standards

Required Qualifications

Essential (ordered by priority)

  1. At least 3+ college years in Computer Science majoring in Cybersecurity
  2. Strong understanding of network, operating systems, and firewalls
  3. Analytical and problem-solving skills; attention to detail
  4. Basic familiarity with apps such as Github and Jira

Non-essential but preferable

  • Experience with cloud security (AWS, Azure) and endpoint protection solutions
  • Knowledge of regulatory compliance standards (ISO 27001, GDPR)

Salary range: 15M montly

Above is expected salary when applying, salary is expected to change after the first month depending on ability and efficency, wage raise applications are also available to be filed after four month period.

Report to: Team leader

Be available on call during non-working hours
