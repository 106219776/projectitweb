IT Support Technician

Reference number: IT123

Overview

Providing direct technical support for staff devices, accounts, and office systems. You will also be logging, troubleshooting, and ensuring minimal time lost due to technical issues.

Job type: Full time

Key Responsibilities

  • Be ready for on-call and walk-up issues
  • Document all changes and support using the company-issued app
  • Install, configure, and maintain laptops, printers, and other equipment if needed
  • Resolve issues and take clear steps and diagnostics to prevent recurring problems

Required Qualifications

Essential (ordered by priority)

  1. At least 3+ years of college in Computer Science
  2. Working knowledge of Windows 10/11 and basic networking (TCP/IP, DNS, etc.)
  3. Clear communication and a problem-solving mindset
  4. Basic familiarity with apps such as GitHub and Jira

Non-essential but preferable

  • Ability to integrate easily with employee groups
  • Basic scripting for automation

Salary range: 10M monthly

The above is the expected salary when applying. Salary may change after the first month depending on ability and efficiency. Wage raise applications can also be filed after a four-month period.

Report to: Team Leader

*Be available on call during non-working hours

*Flexible schedule

Cybersecurity Specialist

Reference number: CS123

Overview

Protecting the company’s, employees’, and customers’ applications from unwanted malware and cyber threats. You will be in charge of monitoring, detecting, and responding to any security breaches while maintaining optimal standards.

Job type: Full time

Key Responsibilities

  • Update and maintain the network’s security and integrity
  • Monitor to detect potential threats and attacks
  • Train employees on safe cybersecurity practices
  • Develop and enforce security policies, guidelines, and standards

Required Qualifications

Essential (ordered by priority)

  1. At least 3+ years of college in Computer Science majoring in Cybersecurity
  2. Strong understanding of networks, operating systems, and firewalls
  3. Analytical and problem-solving skills; attention to detail
  4. Basic familiarity with apps such as GitHub and Jira

Non-essential but preferable

  • Experience with cloud security (AWS, Azure) and endpoint protection solutions
  • Knowledge of regulatory compliance standards (ISO 27001, GDPR)

Salary range: 15M monthly

The above is the expected salary when applying. Salary may change after the first month depending on ability and efficiency. Wage raise applications can also be filed after a four-month period.

Report to: Team Leader

*Be available on call during non-working hours

*Flexible schedule
